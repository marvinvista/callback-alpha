# Call And Close Framework

## Call skeleton

1. Open and align on agenda
2. Discovery
3. Restate pain and stakes
4. Present approach
5. Demo by pain area
6. Trial close
7. Pricing
8. Direct ask

## Discovery question set

- What are you trying to improve right now?
- How do you handle it today?
- Where does the current approach break down?
- What happens if nothing changes?
- Who else will care about this decision?

## Trial close prompts

- Does this look relevant to the problem you described?
- Is this the sort of outcome you were hoping to see?
- Based on what we have covered, does this look worth progressing?

## Objection handling model

1. Name the objection clearly
2. Confirm you understand it
3. Respond with business logic or evidence
4. Check whether the objection is now resolved
5. Return to the close

## Proposal packaging

When the buyer is interested but scope is flexible:

- present two or three sensible options
- recommend one
- explain the tradeoff in business terms
- make the next step explicit
