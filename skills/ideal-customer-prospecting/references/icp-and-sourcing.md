## Positive signal categories

- Hiring or growth signal
- Tooling signal
- Process complexity signal
- Revenue or risk signal
- Team specialization signal
- Public urgency signal

## Disqualifier categories

- Wrong workflow
- Wrong team structure
- Too early
- Too small to realize value
- Too mature for the use case
- Missing technical prerequisite
- No plausible buyer or user

## Simple scoring model

Score each account 0-2 on:

- Pain fit
- Team fit
- Urgency
- Tooling or process sophistication
- Reachable contact structure

Interpretation:

- `8-10`: pursue now
- `5-7`: pursue if capacity exists
- `0-4`: disqualify or revisit later

## Discovery questions

- How do you handle this workflow today?
- Who feels the pain most directly?
- How often does the problem show up?
- What happens when the current process breaks down?
- Who would need to be involved to change this?
- What tools or services are already in place?

## First-list checklist

- Prioritize accounts with explicit need signals.
- Balance obvious fits with a few edge cases worth testing.
- Capture why each target made the list.
- Capture what still needs discovery before a serious sales motion.
