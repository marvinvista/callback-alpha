# Inbound Triage

## Routing matrix

- `Hot`: strong fit plus explicit interest
- `Warm`: plausible fit but incomplete information
- `Unclear`: little context, unclear pain or role
- `Disqualified`: clear mismatch

## Fast response rule

Reply with:

1. acknowledgement
2. fit-based next step
3. no more than three discovery questions if needed

## Discovery prompts

- What problem are you trying to solve right now?
- Who would use this first?
- What does your current process look like?
- What timing are you working toward?

## Response patterns

### Hot

- confirm fit
- offer a meeting
- include one short sentence on likely value

### Warm

- acknowledge request
- ask for missing qualification details
- offer a call if easier

### Unclear

- thank them
- ask a minimal clarifying question set
- avoid long product copy

### Disqualified

- decline politely
- state the mismatch briefly
- leave the door open if their situation changes
