# Readiness Checklist

## Minimum exit criteria

- More than one seller can produce acceptable results
- The sales motion is documented well enough to teach
- Core metrics exist and are reviewed regularly
- Hiring profile is clear
- Onboarding path exists
- Stage definitions and basic process discipline exist

## Signals you are not ready

- One person is still the only person who can really sell
- Product narrative changes every week without a stable core
- Qualification is inconsistent
- Pipeline and activity tracking are weak
- New hires still depend on constant improvisation from one person

## When a team lead may be enough

- The team is small
- One function needs day-to-day support
- Formal people management still sits elsewhere
- The problem is local coaching, not broad organizational design

## Early manager profile

Prefer:

- hands-on operator
- current-scale relevance
- comfort with metrics and coaching
- direct familiarity with a similar deal motion

Be careful of:

- large-company title inflation
- leaders used to big support layers
- candidates who have not managed inside a similar stage and complexity

## First 90-day priorities

1. learn the motion
2. stabilize metrics and process
3. reinforce onboarding and coaching
4. improve forecast and pipeline discipline
5. help hiring without rewriting everything at once
