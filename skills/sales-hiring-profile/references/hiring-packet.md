# Hiring Packet

## Core scorecard fields

- Role fit
- Learning speed
- Resourcefulness
- Activity orientation
- Communication quality
- Evidence of ownership

## Interview loop

1. Intro screen
2. Role-fit interview
3. Practical exercise or mock
4. Team or values interview
5. Close interview

## Practical exercise ideas

- SDR: cold-call mock or outbound email rewrite
- AE: discovery plus demo segment
- CS: onboarding plan or renewal-risk review
- Manager: metric diagnosis plus coaching plan

## Red flags

- Wants title without role clarity
- Can only talk in generic sales clichés
- Cannot explain how they learn a new market
- Avoids accountability for outcomes
- Strong pedigree but weak fit for the actual motion

## Offer checklist

- Role scope is explicit
- Compensation logic is clear
- Start date is real
- Early ramp expectations are visible
- Candidate knows why this role matters now
