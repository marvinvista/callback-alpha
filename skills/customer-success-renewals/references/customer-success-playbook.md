# Customer Success Playbook

## Outcome template

- Customer goal:
- Timeframe:
- Success metric:
- Owner:
- Risk if not achieved:

## Onboarding checklist

- Confirm scope
- Confirm users and owner
- Set milestone dates
- Establish first success signal
- Schedule follow-up

## Health review prompts

- Is the core workflow live?
- Is the main stakeholder engaged?
- Is value observable yet?
- Are there adoption blockers?

## QBR outline

1. Original goal
2. Progress and metrics
3. Wins
4. Risks
5. Next quarter priorities

## Renewal timing

- `120-90 days out`: health review and risk scan
- `90-60 days out`: value recap and stakeholder alignment
- `60-30 days out`: confirm path to renewal
- `30 days out`: finalize terms and next-year plan
